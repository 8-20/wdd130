Why not try tubing down the river?

We offer the following activities:

Scenic Float

This is our most popular event. Enjoy a relaxing day floating down one of our beautiful rivers with breath-taking views. We suggest several exceptonal locations. You may choose to hire a guide or float on your own. We provide all the necessary equipment.

White-Water Tubing

If you prefer a bit more excitement, why not try some white-water tubing? We have several locations that are challenging, but safe. All the necessary safety equipment is available to rent. We recommend an experienced guide for these more challenging excursions.

Want to stop in a location of your choice along the river and enjoy a delicious lunch? Just choose one of the lunches from our menu. Your lunch will travel along with you in its own waterproof container. Stop anywhere you wish and enjoy your lunch on the river.

Want to extend the fun? Why not make it an overnight excursion and camp along the river at a spot of your choice. We can also include a delicious dinnner and breakfast to make your experience even more perfect.
